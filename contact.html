Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our fantasy sports mobile game

What games can I play?

Our fantasy sports platform currently offers football fantasy games where you can create your dream team, compete with other players, and win exciting prizes based on real-world football matches.

How do I start playing?

Simply download our app from the Google Play Store, create an account, and you can start building your fantasy teams right away. We provide detailed tutorials and guides to help you get started.

Is the game free to play?

Yes, you can download and play the game for free. We offer both free contests and paid contests where you can win real prizes. You can choose which type of contests you want to participate in.

How do rewards work?

You earn points based on how your selected players perform in real matches. The better your team performs, the higher you rank in contests. Top-ranking players win prizes according to the contest rules and prize pool.
