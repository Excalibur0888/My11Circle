Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about fantasy football and our platform

How do I get started with fantasy football?
Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, and join a league or tournament. You can then draft your team, manage your roster, and compete against other players. We provide comprehensive tutorials and guides for beginners.
What football leagues are available on your platform?
Our platform currently offers fantasy leagues for all major football competitions including Premier League, Champions League, La Liga, Bundesliga, and international tournaments.
How do your scoring systems work?
Our scoring system is based on real-world player performance statistics. Points are awarded for various achievements like goals, assists, clean sheets, and other key football metrics. Each competition has its own specific scoring criteria which you can view in the game rules section.
Is it free to play?
Yes, we offer both free and premium contests. You can start playing for free to learn the ropes, and when you're ready, you can participate in premium contests to earn more points and exclusive rewards.
How do you ensure fair play?
We have strict fair play policies and advanced security measures in place. Our system monitors for suspicious activity, and we verify all users to maintain the integrity of competitions. Multiple accounts and collusion are strictly prohibited.
